GDG PUNE

Google Developers Group pune is a drive to concentrate the efforts of many developers in and around the magnificent city of pune to learn, share and get productive using the various Google products. It was founded in 2012.

In order to reach out to more developers on specific domains, GDG pune has inaugurated GCDC on 2018. Also, a special community called Women Techmakers has been founded for Women to bridge the gap between women coming from Science, Technology, Engineering & Management (STEM).

WHAT IS GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, Chrome, Drive, and Google Cloud platforms, to product APIs like the Cast API, Maps API, and YouTube API.

A GDG can take many forms -- from just a few people getting together to watch our latest video, to large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to events like code sprints and hackathons. However, at the core, GDGs are focused on developers and technical content, and the core audience should be developers.

WTM PUNE

A diverse mix of voices leads to better discussions, decisions, and outcomes for everyone. - Sundar Pichai, CEO, Google Inc Diversity comes in different forms and plays a crucial role in uplifting society for better. Women Techmakers Hyderabad is a humble initiative to amplify opportunities for women across the city to learn, share and infuse creativity with a purpose to build a platform for making the contributions of women more visible and adaptable.

Founded in 2013, as Geek Girls Hyderabad, the aim of the group has been to engage more women from different domains and across different ages into developing technological applications and accelerating the involvement of such brilliant minds for sharing their expertise.
